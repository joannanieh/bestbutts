Problem

Cafes and private households throw out bread butts because they do not have a way to use or repurpose them. The issue is rooted in the bread buttsâ€™ inferior taste and texture. This makes them not as usable in cafes where they cannot be used for sandwiches, their core product. The USDA estimates about one-third of all the bread made in United States goes to waste. In their roadmap to less food waste, ReFED(food waste nonprofit) reported that grain products constitute 19 percent of food waste in our country. In New York City, City Harvest redistributed 5.1 million pounds of bread in 2016.

Solution

Best Butts directly addresses the core issue of lack of awareness and creativity in using bread butts. By providing recipes, ideas, and tips, it empowers consumers and cafes to find appealing ways to repurpose bread butts, thus reducing waste. The app can help users immediately upon download, offering instant access to recipes and ideas for repurposing bread butts.

BestButts serves as a centralized platform for users to discover & share bread butt recipes, fostering a vibrant community around sustainable cooking practices.
